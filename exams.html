About

Here you can find the schedule for all of the exams in this course. Remember, you are allowed to miss one (non-final) exam with no questions asked. However, you must reschedule to complete this exam within one week of the exam's administered date; otherwise the exam score will be recorded as a zero.

I will post the exams after every student taken it; so given the policy described above, all exams will be posted within a week after administered. I will also post the solutions to the exams. This will be a very good study resource as the final exam will consist of problems very similar to the ones found here.
